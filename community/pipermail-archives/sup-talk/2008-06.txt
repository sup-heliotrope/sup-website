the 'best' name as cached by PersonManager will be used.  I've been
running this patch for about a week and am happy with it.  A colleague
has also been using it.

> --- RuntimeError from thread: load threads for thread-index-mode
> invalid source 2

It looks like there's a source referenced in your index file that didn't
appear in your sources.yaml. Did you manually edit your list of sources,
or somehow otherwise create this situation? Deleting a source from the
list may also do this.

The quick answer is to regenerate your index from scratch; then
everything should be synced up with your sources.yaml file. Something
like:
   rm -rf ~/.sup/ferret
   sup-sync --all-sources

Of course that may take some time, depending on how much email you have.
